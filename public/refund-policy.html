Our Commitment

OwnThatDomain is committed to providing a fair and transparent purchasing experience. This Refund Policy explains your rights and our obligations in relation to the one-time payment of £9 that unlocks Stages 4 and 5 of the domain negotiation workflow.

What You Are Purchasing

The £9 payment provides immediate digital access to Stages 4 and 5 of the OwnThatDomain workflow. This is a one-time payment for digital content that is delivered instantly upon purchase confirmation. No physical goods are involved and no ongoing subscription is created.

Refund Eligibility

Because the paid content is delivered immediately and digitally upon purchase, we are generally unable to offer refunds once access has been granted. However, we will consider refund requests in the following circumstances:

  • You were charged but access to Stages 4 and 5 was not granted due to a technical fault
  • You were charged more than once for the same purchase
  • Your request is submitted within 14 days of the original purchase date

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please email hello@mitchellsolutions.org with the subject line: OwnThatDomain Refund Request. Please include your name, the email address used at checkout, and a brief description of the issue. We aim to respond to all refund requests within 3 business days.

Processing

Approved refunds will be processed via Lemon Squeezy, our payment provider, back to the original payment method. Processing times are typically 5 to 10 business days depending on your bank or card provider.

Consumer Rights

If you are based in the United Kingdom or European Union, you may have statutory rights under consumer protection legislation, including rights relating to digital content. Nothing in this Refund Policy limits or excludes those statutory rights.
